Mission Statement

Ensure that resources and assistance is available to those who are facing natural disasters. We do this through the collaboration of volunteers, organizations, and everyday civilians. With a strong commitment to improving the lives of those in a crisis, we strive to extend care and support to those individuals affected.

Made With Care

Reach&Relief was born of a desire to bring assistance to prevent and alleviate human suffering wherever it may be found. Its purpose is to protect life and health and to ensure respect for the human being. It promotes mutual understanding, friendship, cooperation and lasting peace amongst all peoples.

We aspire to turn compassion into action so that all people affected by disaster receive care, shelter and hope. So that our communities are ready and prepared for disasters. So that and in an emergency, there are always trained individuals nearby, ready to save lives.
